Welcome to Sunsari Technical

Nestled in the vibrant city of Dharan, Nepal, Public High School is a distinguished community government institution dedicated to providing quality education to over 4,500 students. Our school is a cornerstone of academic excellence and holistic development, emphasizing the importance of nurturing each student's unique potential.

At Public High School, our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive learning environment. Our experienced and passionate faculty deliver a comprehensive curriculum that not only meets national standards but also fosters critical thinking, creativity, and social responsibility. We believe that education extends beyond the classroom, so we offer a diverse array of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and cultural programs. This ensures our students develop into well-rounded individuals equipped for the challenges of the modern world.
